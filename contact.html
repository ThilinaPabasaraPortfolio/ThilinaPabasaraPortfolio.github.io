Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time depends on the complexity and scope of the project. Simple graphic designs can be completed in 1-3 days, while complex 3D modeling projects may take 1-2 weeks. Rush services are available for urgent projects.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes, I include 2-3 rounds of revisions with every project to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.

What file formats do you deliver?

I deliver files in industry-standard formats based on your needs: AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, JPG for graphics; MP4, MOV, AVI for videos; OBJ, FBX, BLEND for 3D models. Source files are included when requested.

How do you handle payments?

I typically work with a 50% upfront payment and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, milestone-based payments can be arranged. I accept bank transfers and popular digital payment methods.

Can you work with my existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! I can work within your existing brand guidelines and style requirements. Just share your brand book, color palettes, and any specific requirements during our initial consultation.
